# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to maintain a welcoming, professional, and collaborative environment.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in all interactions
- Focus on constructive feedback
- Keep discussions technical and relevant
- Assume good intent

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment of any kind
- Disruptive or off-topic discussions
- Disrespect toward contributors or maintainers

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary restriction
- Removal from collaboration

---

## Scope

Applies to all:
- Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Code reviews
